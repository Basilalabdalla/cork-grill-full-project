Our Story

Welcome to Cork Grill

Nestled in the vibrant Victorian Quarter at 20 Coburg St, Cork Grill is more than just a restaurant; it's a celebration of flavor, community, and the art of grilling. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a welcoming space where people can enjoy high-quality, delicious food made with passion.

We believe in using the freshest local ingredients whenever possible, ensuring that every dish we serve bursts with authentic taste. Our chefs are masters of the grill, transforming simple ingredients into culinary delights that will tantalize your taste buds.

Our Philosophy

Quality Ingredients: We source the best produce and meats, prioritizing local suppliers to support our community and guarantee freshness.

Passionate Cooking: Every meal is prepared with care and attention to detail, reflecting our love for great food.

Warm Hospitality: We strive to make every guest feel like part of the Cork Grill family. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, we look forward to welcoming you to Cork Grill and sharing our passion for food with you.
